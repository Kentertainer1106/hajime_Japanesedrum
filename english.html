What We Are

After long years of experience in Japanese drum,

5 of us decided to team up and play for fun.

We named ourselves
玄-Hajime.

It sounds the same as the word 'beginning'.

We do not wear the same costume.

Because each individual sparkles the most when they show their uniqueness.
